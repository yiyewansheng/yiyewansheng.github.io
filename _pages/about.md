I am currently at Stanford University HAI, currently working on spatial intelligence, audio-driven speaking face generation, text-to-speech, and music generation. If you are looking for any form of academic collaboration, please feel free to email me at yiyewansheng@gmail.com. We are hiring interns! I graduated from Ningxia University with a bachelor's degree; and from Nanyang Technological University with a master's degree in Computer Science and Technology. I am currently studying at Stanford University School of Computer Science and expect to receive my doctorate in June 2029. From July 2022 to July 2023, I served as a quality engineer for Fu Di Battery, the second business department of BYD Auto Co., Ltd. From July 2023 to present, I have served as the CEO of GuaGuaniu, dedicated to exploring the technology and practical applications of artificial intelligence. My research interests include spatial intelligence, 3D generation, video generation, speech synthesis, neural machine translation, and automatic music generation. I have published many papers at top international artificial intelligence conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, KDD, etc. In order to promote the study and exchange of artificial intelligence technology in China and the international community, we (with 11 other young scholars around the world) established the Global Artificial Intelligence Response Institute in 2021. I am honored to serve as one of the committee chairmen.
